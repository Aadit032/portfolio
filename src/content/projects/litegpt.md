A compact GPT-style model trained from scratch with a focus on architecture and training intuition.

## What it is

LiteGPT is a small-scale language model experiment built to understand the trade-offs behind architecture choices, data mix, and training setup.

## What I learned

- 25M parameters is enough to explore the basics of pretraining behavior.
- Data quality matters as much as model design.
- Simple ablations are extremely valuable when learning from first principles.
